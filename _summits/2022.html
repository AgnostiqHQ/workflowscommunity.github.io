November 29 and 30, 2022

8am-11am PST / 11am-2pm EST / 16:00-19:00 CEST

In the 2022 edition of the Workflows Community Summit, we will revisit the 2021 community roadmap, discuss how things have evolved and propose an updated roadmap. This unique event invites researchers and users of workflow management systems, and will seek to identify crucial challenges in the workflow community.
